Change Interceptor

The Change Interceptor is responsible for intercepting and if required filtering changes on the shared ontology. Filters are be based on the node a change originates from and also the kind of change.

This component is local on a node. After a change has been intercepted it is either modified and forwarded to the change analyzer or filtered.
